What is a dwarf planet?

Pluto & Dwarf Planets A dwarf planet is a celestial body in our Solar System that orbits the Sun, is massive enough to be roughly spherical due to its own gravity, but has not cleared its orbital path of other objects and is not a moon



Pluto was considered a planet until August 24, 2006 when it was reconsidered to be a dwarf planet and hence not part of the solar system planets
